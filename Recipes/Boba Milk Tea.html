Boba Milk Tea Recipe

We will learn how to make boba milk tea!

This is a classic drink originating from Taiwan. It uses a combination of milk and tea

Required Ingredients

  • Milk (Or Any Alternatives)
  • Black Tea
  • Tapioca
  • sugar (If wanted)

Steps to Make the drink!

  1. Brew the Tea with hot water
  2. Boil the tapiocsa balls and mix into a bronw sugar mixture
  3. Pour ice into a glass
  4. Add milk halfway to the glass
  5. Add tapioca Pearls
  6. Add brewed tea
  7. Stir anf enjoy!
